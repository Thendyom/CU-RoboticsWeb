Research

Prepare before asking for supervision

Read recent group publications, choose a concrete method or application area, and be ready to explain what you can implement or evaluate. Good thesis conversations start with a specific technical question.

Thesis

Define the scope early

A useful robotics thesis has a clear boundary: one method, one platform or data source, one evaluation plan. Broad ideas become research when they can be turned into an experiment that can actually be run.

Academic Work

What good student work looks like

Good projects are narrow enough to finish, documented enough to reproduce, and honest about limitations. For robotics, that usually means clear experimental conditions, checked data, working code, and a written argument that explains what changed and why it matters.

Students should also expect a lot of ordinary engineering work: debugging, documenting assumptions, reading papers slowly, and being precise about what the robot or model did not do.
