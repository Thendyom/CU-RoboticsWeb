Research Group

Marine Systems and Robotics

Prof. Maurelli's group develops intelligent capabilities for autonomous platforms, with emphasis on positioning and navigation, advanced filtering and data fusion, fault management, and perception.

The group uses inertial, acoustic, video, and LiDAR sensing to support autonomous real-time decision making. Public group information describes a team around Prof. Maurelli with PhD students and lab technical support.

Research themes

  • Autonomous underwater vehicles and surface systems
  • Navigation, localization, SLAM, and sonar perception
  • Fault management for autonomous architectures
  • Marine robotics education and international research partnerships
