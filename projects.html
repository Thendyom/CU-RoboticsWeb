Research

Research across autonomous systems

Constructor Robotics lists work across marine robotics, autonomous driving, response robots, space robotics, logistics, and robotics education. RIS students meet these themes through coursework, labs, projects, internships, and thesis work.

Constructor Robotics spans the application domains below, connecting autonomous-systems methods with demanding real-world settings.
